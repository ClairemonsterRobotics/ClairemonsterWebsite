Contribute

There are many ways to support our team. We are always looking for donations to fund the program, mentors to help our students, and corporate partnerships to expand the experience. We are 501c3 affiliated with the Clairemont High School Foundation meaning that all of your donations are tax deductable. Tax Id #33-0551164.

PayPal

Donate to our PayPal to fund our cause. Your money will go to providing free education on robotics, marketing, and programming to students in the community.

Become A Mentor

Become a mentor to teach and support the team members. Mentors are the core of our team. Their presence is always welcome to help out with building, or designing, or programming, or marketing. Sign up to be a mentor and we will contact you with more information.
Sign up to become a mentor!

Corporate Sponsorships

Corporate sponsorships are a major aspect of FIRST Robotics. That is the source of the majority of our funding. We display the logos of our sponsors on our robot, on our website, and at competition. Sponsors are incredibly helpful as they provide real-world experiences and resources that are impossible for us receive otherwise. We love our sponsors!
